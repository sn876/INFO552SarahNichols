Hangry App

This project consists of storyboarding, wireframing and ultimately prototyping the Hangry app, which automates the process of the restaraunt dining experience. This was completed as part of INFO691 at Drexel University. Wireframes and storyboards included were done by myself. Team members storyboards and wireframes are not included in the gallery.

Voice Activated Retinal Display

This project consists of a full circle design process of a voice-based retinal display techonology. Here I will outline the steps taken from start tro finish that produced a voice-based prototype experience, as well as the rationale for why I chose these options

Zoo App Prototype

Wireframing and clickable prototypes were created to walk the user through the process of purchasing tickets at their local zoo.
