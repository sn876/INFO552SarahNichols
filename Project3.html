Zoo App Prototype

Wireframing and clickable prototypes were created to walk the user through the process of purchasing tickets at their local zoo.

The Drexel Zoo App was created using a functional prototype of an app that allows users to purchase tickets for the Drexel Zoo. The Drexel Zoo clickable prototype leads users through the process of visitors to selecting and purchasing admissions tickets based on dates and times. The wireframes outline the process of ordering tickets to Drexel Zoo. It highlights the layout and ordering process with navigable back buttons that include the process from start to finish. Further detail can be hereon Figma.

After the wireframes were complete and reviewed work began on the clickable prototype. In this prototype, you can see the evolution from the wireframe into the final high-fidelity product. The design evolved to include engaging material while allowing a clickable prototype to guide the user through the process in question. The prototype is meant to outline the process of purchasing general admission tickets. The clickable prototype can be found hereon Figma
