Bio

Hello! I am Sarah, and I am based out of Roanoke, Virginia. Prior to my work as a UX researcher and designer, I worked in the United States Air Force as an aerospace ground equipment technician. After my time in the military, I pursued my education, graduating with a degree in geography and environmental studies with a minor in sustainability. My personal interest in psychology led me to pursue my graduate degree in human-computer interaction and user experience where I can better help companies understand their product visions. In my free time I enjoy getting out into nature, hiking and kayaking.
